<p><p>The Highly Reactive Polyisobutylene market consists of three primary types based on molecular weight: Low, Medium, and High Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene. Low molecular weight polyisobutylene is utilized for adhesives and sealants due to its quick curing properties. Medium molecular weight variants offer enhanced viscosity and flexibility, making them suitable for coatings and lubricants. High molecular weight polyisobutylene provides superior strength and durability for applications like tire manufacturing and automotive components, serving diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
