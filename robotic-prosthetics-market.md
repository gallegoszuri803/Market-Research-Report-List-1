<p><strong>The Robotic Prosthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lower limb robotic prosthetics</li><li>Upper limb robotic prosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic prosthetics market is categorized into two primary types: lower limb and upper limb prosthetics. Lower limb robotic prosthetics focus on aiding mobility and stability for individuals with leg amputations, utilizing advanced sensors and motors to mimic natural walking motions. In contrast, upper limb robotic prosthetics are designed for arm and hand rehabilitation, enhancing dexterity and functionality through adaptable grip patterns and joint movements. Both markets emphasize innovation, user comfort, and integration with neural control systems, significantly improving the quality of life for amputees.</p></p>
