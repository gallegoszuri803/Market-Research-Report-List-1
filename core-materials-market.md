Honeycomb structures, formed from hexagonal cells, provide high stiffness and strength, making them ideal for aerospace, automotive, and building sectors. Balsa wood, a natural core material, is lightweight yet strong, frequently utilized in wind turbine blades and other composite applications for its eco-friendliness and durability.</p></p>
